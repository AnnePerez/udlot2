DRONE

Beat a pair of percussion stick continuously, slowly until the end of the music.

MIXED INSTRUMENTS: GROUP 1 and GROUP 2

For the duration of each cell number, follow the instrument and choose any counting pair displayed in the instruction box.
The player hits, pounds, blows the instrument twice: one at the end of count the 1st number, and another at the end of counting the 2nd number.
Each player should count the numbers at their own speed.
